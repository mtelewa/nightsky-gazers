Our Inspiration

Beyond his scientific contributions, Sagan was a gifted communicator who made complex scientific concepts accessible to the general public. He authored more than 20 books, including the best-selling "Cosmos," which accompanied the highly acclaimed television series of the same name.

In addition to his work in academia, Brian Cox is perhaps best known for his television appearances and documentaries.

Astronomer whose groundbreaking discoveries revolutionized our understanding of the universe and forever changed the field of astronomy. Hubble's most significant contribution to astronomy was the groundbreaking discovery that the universe is expanding. By observing distant galaxies, Hubble found that galaxies are moving away from each other at velocities proportional to their distance. This discovery, known as Hubble's Law, provided compelling evidence for the expanding universe and laid the groundwork for the Big Bang theory.

Who are we?

Our passion for the cosmos and the wonders of the universe drive us to create this platform. We believe that stargazing is not just a hobby but a profound experience that connects us to the vastness of space and the mysteries it holds. We meet at top stargazing sites at the right time with the right equipment.

Why are we doing it?

In today's fast-paced world, it's easy to forget to look up and appreciate the beauty above us. We aim to reignite the spark of curiosity and wonderment in people of all ages by providing a place where they can learn about the stars, planets, and celestial events.
